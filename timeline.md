# Time Line when preparing for the conference


#### 12-18 months

- [General Chair](/organising-committee.html): finalise main chair positions in OC in time for web site launch and OC lunch at prior ISWC. 


#### 12 months (at prior ISWC Conference)

- [General Chair](/organising-committee.html): go to (or send representative to) ISWC to attend *Town Hall*, *OC Lunch* and *Closing Ceremony*. 

- [General Chair](/organising-committee.html): organise **Handover** meeting (one week afetr previous ISWC) with previous and new *Organising Committee* to coordinate handover of materials.  This extends discussion from the *OC Lunch*.
